Powerful Features

Everything You Need to Succeed

A comprehensive suite of tools designed to help you manage your retail business from production to sales.

Inventory Management

Complete control over your inventory across production, stores, and retail shops. Track stock levels, manage batches, and set custom pricing per location.

Low Stock Alerts

Never run out of stock again. Automatic notifications when inventory drops below reorder levels, keeping your shelves stocked and customers happy.

Product Transfer & Tracking

Seamlessly move products from production to stores to shops. Full traceability with dispute handling for quantity discrepancies.

Metrics & Reporting

Gain insights with top 10 product reports, periodic sales analysis, profit/loss tracking by product, location, or manager.

Multi-Payment Methods

Accept Cash, E-Cash (Mobile Money via Paystack), and Credit Sales on customer accounts. Flexible payment options for any customer.

Accountant View

Dedicated dashboard for accountants to track cash flows, manage cash transfers, view price history, and monitor financial health.

Multiple Shops Support

Manage multiple retail locations with the same or different stores. Each shop can have custom pricing and independent inventory.

Auditor View

Read-only access for auditors to review all transactions, inventory movements, transfers, and sales. Complete transparency and accountability.

Shift System

Manage multiple attendants per store with shift tracking. Open/close shifts with cash reconciliation and accountability per user.
